EXP REALTY BRANDING

The website includes the text disclosure “Brokered by eXp Realty.”

To display the eXp logo:
1. Download an approved current logo from the eXp Canva Brand Kit.
2. Save the transparent PNG as assets/exp-realty-logo.png.
3. Have the final use reviewed according to your brokerage's advertising process.

An unofficial or recreated eXp logo has intentionally not been included.

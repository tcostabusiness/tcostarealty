Island communities

Discover your corner of Kauaʻi.

From North Shore calm to South Shore sunshine, each area offers a distinct rhythm and sense of place.

North Shore

Hanalei & Princeville

Mountain views, ocean access, lush landscapes, and a relaxed coastal pace.

East Side

Kapaʻa & Wailua

Convenient island living, beaches, trails, shops, and strong community energy.

Central

Līhuʻe

Everyday convenience, employment centers, airport access, and established neighborhoods.

South Shore

Historic charm, sunny weather, resort amenities, and sought-after coastal living.

West Side

Open landscapes, local character, dramatic sunsets, and a quieter island lifestyle.
